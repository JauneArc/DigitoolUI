What is Digitool?

Digitool is a Social Enterprise facilitating collaboration between clients (business owners), consultants (fledgling entrepreneurs) and training.

CONSULTANT

We create positive employment for young and/or unemployed people who are looking for an opportunity to work in digital marketing. They are our “Consultants”.

CLIENT

Our “Clients” are businesses who need help with their online presence. They need a new website and/or better social media presence at an affordable price point and in a relatively short timeframe.

Affordable cost

Clients are offered services such as Website Design and Development and Social Media Management at relatively low prices, and consultants can get paid while learning jobs.

GET ONLINE

Consultants gain employment, learn, grow and build confidence. Clients improved online presence and more visibility and customers.
